Replace Character

Write a function that replaces all occurrences of a character with another.

Example:
Input: "hello", 'l', 'x'
Output: "hexxo"

Input: "java", 'a', 'o'
Output: "jovo"

Input: "test", 't', 'p'
Output: "pesp"

Input: "abc", 'd', 'e'
Output: "abc"

Note: Replace all occurrences of oldChar with newChar.
